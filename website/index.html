/ 02 — How we work

Five stages.
One partner, start to finish.

No junior associates. No handoffs. When you sign with us, you sign with a partner whose name is on the door — and who will sit across from every buyer in every room.
01
Discovery
Weeks of conversation, not meetings. We learn your business the way you learned it — by asking the same questions, over and over, until the picture is unmistakable.
02
Positioning
The CIM. The teaser. The financial model. Every document is written in-house by the partner running your deal. Never outsourced. Never templated.
03
Quiet Outreach
A shortlist of 40–120 buyers. Every approach NDA'd. Your customers, employees, and competitors never learn the company is for sale.
04
Competitive Process
Management meetings, IOIs, LOIs. We orchestrate tension without theatrics — so the highest bidder is also the right fit for what you've built.
05
Close
Diligence, purchase agreement, escrow, wire. We've closed through bank collapses, pandemics, and two recessions. The steps don't change.
